The Bottom Line

The AI sequence's lower conversion rate (0.9% vs 1.5%) appears to be driven by a significant drop in rep follow-up effort: reps make 41% fewer calls per prospect on the AI sequence. They're loading more prospects in and relying on personalised emails, but not calling at the same rate. Each AI prospect receives 23% fewer total touches (3.13 vs 4.05). When the same 34 reps are compared on both sequences, they book meetings at an identical rate: 0.21 per active week.

Why This Matters

AI's lower conversion may not be a content problem — it's a call discipline problem. The AI rep is busier (91.9 touches/week vs 47.8) but managing 3.2x more prospects, so each prospect gets less attention. More volume, less depth. If call effort were maintained at Traditional levels while also getting AI-personalised emails, conversion could improve significantly.

Interpretation

There is a clear correlation between call effort and AI sequence performance. When reps actually call at a comparable rate on AI, the combination of personalised emails plus calls outperforms Traditional. When they rely on emails alone, Traditional wins. The AI emails are not the problem — the missing calls are.

Actions Recommended Next Steps

▼
Overall Assessment

The AI sequence hasn't been given a fair test yet. Reps changed their behaviour — they stopped calling at the same rate — so we're comparing AI emails alone against Traditional emails plus full call effort. The technology isn't the bottleneck; the execution is. Fix the call discipline and we'll know whether AI personalisation actually moves the needle.

  1. 1
    Enforce call discipline on the AI sequence — mandate same calls-per-prospect targets as Traditional. Reps should treat AI emails as an enhancement to their calling, not a replacement.
  2. 2
    Monitor per-prospect touch rates weekly — track calls/prospect and touches/prospect, not just total activity volume. A rep doing 90 touches/week means nothing if they're spread across 30 prospects at 3 touches each.
  3. 3
    Re-evaluate after 4-6 weeks of enforced discipline — once call effort is normalised, we'll have a clean comparison of whether AI personalisation genuinely improves conversion on top of standard call effort.
  4. 4
    Consider the optimal combination — AI personalised emails + Traditional call effort could be the best of both worlds. The data suggests AI does add value downstream (more pipeline per meeting), so combining AI's content with Traditional's execution cadence may be the winning formula.
